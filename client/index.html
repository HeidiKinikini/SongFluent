Why Use SongFluent?

Every songwriter at some point will encounter a "writers block" or a "song block". We want to help song artists become "SongFluent" again. SongFluent serves as a tool to help influence artists to write beautiful compositions of music again by randomly generating a chord progression in any key of choice. This will hopefully start to get the music in your head flowing again. Or maybe you just want a different idea that you havent thought of! So go ahead click generate and keep going until you feel inspired and find one you like. Then make it your own!
